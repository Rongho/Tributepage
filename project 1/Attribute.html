Records

  • Sourav is the only cricketer to win four consecutive Man of the Match awards in ODIs.
  • He is the ninth-highest run-scorer in ODI history (11363) and third among Indians after Sachin Tendulkar and Virat Kohli.
  • He is one of the six cricketers to have achieved the unique treble of 10,000 runs, 100 wickets & 100 catches in ODI cricket.
  • He scored 183 runs in Cricket World Cup, the highest individual score by an Indian batsman.

Awards and Achievements

  • He was awarded with Padma Shri in 2004
  • He got the Arjuna award for cricket in the year 1997.
  • He was the President of the Cricket Association of Bengal from 2015-2019
  • In October 2019 he became President of the Board of Control for Cricket in India
